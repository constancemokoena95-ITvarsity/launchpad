Welcome to the Paballong Primary School website.

We are a small, one form entry primary school nestled in a beautiful Paballong village just a few minutes drive from Phuthaditjhaba, Bolata, Monontsha and Bluegumbush.

We bring together traditional values, such as self-discipline, perseverance, politeness and educational attainment with modern approaches to encourage independence, leadership, good citizenship and healthy life choices.

We hope you enjoy browsing our website which contains lots of information about our wonderful school. If you have any further enquiries, please feel free to telephone the school office on 058 713 6063.

If you are considering sending your child to Paballong Primary, then we would be very happy to show you around and give you a tour of our happy, friendly school.
