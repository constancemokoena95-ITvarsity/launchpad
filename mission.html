Our Mission

It is the mission and aim of the Paballong Governing Body, the Educators, Staff and Parents to:

  • Provide a holistic education for all children in the community, regardless of race, sex, religions background or economic background.
  • To involve, develop and educate parents to assist in their children's education.
  • To emphasise, within the community that education is community based.

    • It is their right and duty to ensure that children affordedthe opportunity to receive an education.
