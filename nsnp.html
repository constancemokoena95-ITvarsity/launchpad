NATIONAL SCHOOL NUTRION PROGRAMME(NSNP)

The National School Nutrion Programme is the government programme tha provides one nutritious meal to all learners in poorer primary ans secondary school.

The objective is to provide nutritious meals to learners so as to improve their ability to learn.

The programme also teaches learners and parents how to lead a healthy lifestyle, and promote development of school vegetable gardens.
