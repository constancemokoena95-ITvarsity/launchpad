About us

Marvel's The Avengers, also known as The Avangers and Avangers Assemble, is a 2012 superhero film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the name.

The film was released on April 25,2012 internationally and on May 4, 2012 in the united states.

The film spawned three sequels:

Avengers: Age of Ultron in 2015,

Avengers:Infinity War in 2018, and

Avengers: Endgame in 2019.
